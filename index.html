Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At rishtas.in, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you register, we collect personal details including your name, age, gender, location, photographs, religion, caste, educational background, occupation, contact number, and profile preferences. This information is necessary to create your profile and facilitate meaningful connections.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for matchmaking purposes within this platform. We display your profile to other registered users based on gender and preference filters. We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to the core service.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is encrypted and stored securely in a Google database infrastructure. Photographs are compressed before upload to optimize storage and performance. We employ industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your phone number is visible only to users with whom you have mutually accepted an interest. Under no circumstances is your contact information disclosed to unmatched users.

Local Data Caching

To enhance performance and reduce loading times, profile data is cached locally in your browser using encrypted storage. This cached data is automatically cleared upon logout. We recommend using the same browser for an optimal experience.

Data Retention

Accounts that remain inactive for 7 consecutive days are automatically deleted along with all associated data, including profile information, photographs, interests, and shortlists. This policy exists to maintain user privacy and keep the platform active.

Your Rights

You have the right to view, edit, or permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Profile section. Upon account deletion, all your information is immediately and irreversibly removed from our systems.

Password Security

Passwords are stored as one-way hashes and are never stored in plain text. Password resets are limited to once every 7 days for security purposes. We strongly recommend using a unique, strong password for your account.

Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, data requests, or questions about this policy, please contact us at info.rishtas@gmail.com
